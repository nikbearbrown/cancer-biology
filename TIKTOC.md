# Cancer Biology
## Tik TOC Research Spine

**Book type:** Course textbook
**Status:** Split from `cancer-biology-all`; research pass scaffold

## Core Thesis

Cancer Biology teaches cancer as a breakdown of normal cellular regulation: mutation, epigenetic change, altered signaling, failed checkpoints, evasion of death, and metabolic rewiring create cell lineages that evolve inside tissues.

## Primary Reader

Students in biology, biomedical science, pre-med, biotechnology, or health sciences who need a coherent mechanistic introduction before moving into diagnosis, treatment, research methods, nanomedicine, or imaging.

## Learning Arc

1. Establish normal cellular regulation.
2. Show how cancer deregulates genes, epigenetic state, risk exposure, and infection-driven pathways.
3. Build the control systems: cell cycle, apoptosis, and metabolism.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can explain major cancer mechanisms, distinguish genetic from epigenetic and environmental causes, and reason from a molecular failure to a cancer phenotype.
